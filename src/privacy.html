Privacy Policy

Effective:

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how this Trello Power‑Up (the “Service”) processes information when you use it to render Mermaid diagrams on Trello cards. We aim to collect the minimum necessary data to operate the Service.

Information We Process

  • Diagram content: The Service reads Trello card descriptions to find code blocks labeled ```mermaid for the sole purpose of rendering diagrams in your browser.

What We Don’t Collect

  • No analytics, tracking cookies, or profiling.
  • No user account data beyond what Trello provides to the iframe sandbox.
  • No server‑side storage of your diagrams or card content by this Service.

Third‑Party Services

  • Trello Power‑Up Platform: The Service runs within Trello. Your use is also subject to Trello/Atlassian terms and privacy policies.
  • Mermaid Library CDN: Mermaid is loaded from a public CDN. Requests to the CDN are subject to the CDN provider’s policies.

Data Retention

The Service does not persist your diagram content on its own servers and does not store any settings or personal data.

Security

The Service follows Mermaid’s recommended security modes when rendering diagrams (e.g., disabling potentially unsafe features) and avoids storing sensitive data.

Your Choices

  • Disable or remove the Power‑Up at any time from your board.
  • Remove Mermaid code blocks from card descriptions to stop rendering.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, please contact the maintainer of this Power‑Up.
